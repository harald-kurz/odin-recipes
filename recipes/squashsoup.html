Roasted Butternut Squash Soup

Description

This roasted butternut squash soup is my favorite fall soup of all time. It's inexpensive, easy, nutritious, and absolutely delicious!

If you're feeling like something a bit more substantial, try this soup topped with a handful of crispy bacon or diced ham.
